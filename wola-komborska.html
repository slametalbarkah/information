Wola Komborska

Wola Komborska [ˈvɔla kɔmˈbɔrska] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Korczyna, within Krosno County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) east of Korczyna, 13 km (8 mi) north-east of Krosno, and 35 km (22 mi) south of the regional capital Rzeszów.
