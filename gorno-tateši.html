Gorno Tateši

Gorno Tateši (Macedonian: Горно Татеши, Albanian: Tatesh i Epërm) is a village in the municipality of Struga, North Macedonia.





== Name ==

The name of the village is an Albanian toponym and derived possibly from a personal name.





== Demographics ==

As of the 2021 census, Gorno Tateši had 619 residents with the following ethnic composition:



Albanians 601

Persons for whom data are taken from administrative sources 15

Others 2

Macedonians 1

According to the 2002 census, the village had a total of 1148 inhabitants.
