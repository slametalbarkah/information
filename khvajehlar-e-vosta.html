Khvajehlar-e Vosta

Khvajehlar-e Vosta (Persian: خواجه لروسطي, also Romanized as Khvājehlar-e Vosţá) is a village in Charuymaq-e Jonubesharqi Rural District, Shadian District, Charuymaq County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was at 115, in 14 families.
