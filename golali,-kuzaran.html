Golali, Kuzaran

Golali (Persian: گلالي, also Romanized as Golālī) is a village in Haft Ashiyan Rural District, Kuzaran District, Kermanshah County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 130, in 31 families.
