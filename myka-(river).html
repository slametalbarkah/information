Myka (river)

The Myka (Russian: Мыка) is a river in Perm Krai, Russia, a right tributary of the Uls, which in turn is a tributary of the Vishera. The river is 18 kilometres (11 mi) long. It flows into the Uls 27 kilometres (17 mi) from the larger river's mouth.
