Hajjiabad, Bazoft

Hajjiabad (Persian: حاجي اباد, also Romanized as Ḩājjīābād) is a village in Doab Rural District, Bazoft District, Kuhrang County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 74, in 14 families. The village is populated by Lurs.
