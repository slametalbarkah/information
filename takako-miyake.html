Takako Miyake

Takako Miyake (三宅 貴子, Miyake Takako, born 22 July 1974) is a retired Japanese athlete who specialised in the javelin throw. She twice competed at World Championships failing to qualify for the final round. With a personal best of 61.15 metres, she is a former Japanese record holder in the event.
