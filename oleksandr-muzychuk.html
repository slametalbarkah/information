Oleksandr Muzychuk

Oleksandr Muzychuk (born January 10, 1994) is a Ukrainian former footballer who played as a midfielder.





== Club career ==





=== Early career ===

Muzychuk began his youth training as a pupil with Podillya Khmelnytskyi. In 2011, he joined the professional ranks by playing abroad in the Moldovan National Division with Nistru Otaci for two seasons. He returned to his native country in 2013 to play with Zbruch Volochysk in the Ukrainian Football Amateur League. After a season in Ukraine, he returned to the Moldovan top-tier league to play with Costuleni. Following his second stint in Moldova, he returned to his former club academy club Podillya Khmelnytskyi in 2016 to compete in Ukraine's national amateur circuit.





=== Canada ===

Muzychuk spent the summer of 2016 abroad in the Canadian Soccer League with Ukraine United. In his debut season in the Canadian circuit, he helped the club secure a playoff berth by finishing second in the league's first division. He contributed a goal against the Brantford Galaxy in the opening round of the playoffs that advanced the Ukrainians to the next round. Their playoff campaign would conclude in the next round after a defeat by the Serbian White Eagles.
