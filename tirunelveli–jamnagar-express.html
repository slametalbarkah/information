Tirunelveli–Jamnagar Express

The 19577 / 19578 Tirunelveli–Jamnagar Express is an express train operated by Indian Railways that runs between Tirunelveli Junction and Jamnagar in India. It is currently being operated with 19577/19578 train numbers on bi-weekly basis.

It operates as train number 19577 from Tirunelveli Junction to Jamnagar and as train number 19578 from Jamnagar to Tirunelveli Junction.





== Coach composition ==

The train has standard LHB rakes with max speed of 130 kmph. The train consists of 24 coaches :



1 AC II Tier

5 AC III Tier

5 Ac three tier economy

10 Sleeper Coaches

4 General

2 EoG Seating cum Luggage Rake





== Service ==

The 19577/Tirunelveli–Jamnagar Express has an average speed of 55 km/h and covers 2434 km in 44 hrs 25 mins.

The 19578/Jamnagar–Tirunelveli Express has an average speed of 54 km/h and covers 2434 km in 45 hrs 00 mins.





== Route & halts ==

The 19577/19578 Tirunelveli–Jamnagar Express runs from Tirunelveli Junction via

Vallioor

Nagercoil Town,

Thiruvananthapuram Central,

Kollam Junction, Ernakulam Junction, Shoranur Junction, Mangaluru Junction, Madgaon Junction, Ratnagiri, Panvel Junction, Vasai Road, Vadodara Junction, Ahmedabad Junction, Surendranagar Junction, Rajkot Junction to Jamnagar.





== Schedule ==





== Traction ==

It is now hauled by a Vadodara Loco Shed based WAP-5 / WAP-7 electric locomotive on its entire journey.





== Rake sharing ==

The train shares its rake with 22907 / 22908 Madgaon-Hapa Superfast Express.
