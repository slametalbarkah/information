Neyzeh Rud

Neyzeh Rud (Persian: نيزه رود, also Romanized as Neyzeh Rūd) is a village in Posht-e Arbaba Rural District, Alut District, Baneh County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 307, in 59 families. The village is populated by Kurds.
