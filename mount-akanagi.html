Mount Akanagi

Mount Akanagi (赤椰岳, Akanagidake) is a mountain of the Kiso Mountains and located on the border between Okuwa, Kiso District, and Iijima, Kamiina District, Nagano Prefecture, in the Chūbu region of Japan. Its peak is 2,798 m (9,180 ft) high.
