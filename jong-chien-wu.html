Jong Chien-wu

Jong Chien-wu (Chinese: 鍾劍武) is a Taiwanese football (soccer) manager. He has managed Lukuang football team, Chinese Taipei national futsal team, etc. He is now Chinese Taipei Football Association's deputy general secretary.
